# GG: Go Goon — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

---

## The Short Version

GG does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Ever.

---

## Data Collection

GG collects no personal information. No account is required to use the app. No analytics, advertising networks, or third-party tracking SDKs are used.

## Local Storage

All app data — including session history, streak counts, and notification settings — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## Notifications

GG uses local scheduled notifications only. These are generated and delivered entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to or processed by any external server.

## External Links

The app contains a link to [buymeacoffee.com](https://buymeacoffee.com/gusfromspace). Tapping this link opens an external website subject to their own privacy policy. GG has no control over and assumes no responsibility for external sites.

## Children

GG is intended for adults 18 and older and is not directed at children. Do not use this app if you are under 18.

## Changes

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will reflect that. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
